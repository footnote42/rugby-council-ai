# Trojans RFC Coaching Framework

## Club Vision & Ethos

**Vision:** "To support every player to develop a passion for a lifetime of rugby"

**Ethos:** "To be an inclusive club, offering opportunities for ALL players to enjoy and develop the skills, behaviours and values rugby offers"

## The Trojans Player Framework

What we develop in every player across three dimensions:

### BEHAVIOURS
- Teamwork
- Resilience
- Enjoyment
- Discipline
- Sportsmanship
- Creativity
- Awareness
- Decision making
- Self-organising

### SKILLS
- Agility
- Balance
- Coordination
- Footwork
- Pass/catch
- Tackling
- Fitness
- Body control
- Core strength
- Flexibility

### KNOWLEDGE
- Offside
- Run forward/pass back
- Scrum
- Lineout
- Playing positions
- Defensive line
- Attacking shape
- Kicking
- Age group rules
- Ruck/maul

## Trojans Coaching Habits

Five essential elements that MUST be present in every session:

### 1. Shared Purpose
- Session has clear aim linked to Trojans Player elements
- Purpose is shared with players at start of session
- Players understand WHY they're doing each activity

### 2. Progression
- Each activity starts achievable for ALL players
- Progressions add challenge gradually
- Use STEP principle (Space, Task, Equipment, People)

### 3. Praise
- Specific praise linked to purpose and TREDS values
- Give examples of what to praise in each activity
- Reinforce positive behaviors and skill development

### 4. Review
- Question-based reviews during activities
- Encourage player-led reflection
- Check understanding throughout session

### 5. Choice
- Give players options/ownership in activities
- Specify where choices can be given
- Build player autonomy and engagement

## TREDS Values

Core values that underpin all coaching and behavior:

- **T**eamwork
- **R**espect (added to complement Discipline for sportsmanship)
- **E**njoyment
- **D**iscipline
- **S**portsmanship

## Session Design Principles - APES

All sessions must be:

- **Active** - Players engaged and moving
- **Purposeful** - Clear links to development goals
- **Enjoyable** - Fun and engaging for all abilities
- **Safe** - Age-appropriate and risk-managed

## Integration with RFU Frameworks

The Trojans framework integrates with:
- RFU Regulation 15 (age-grade rules)
- RFU Principles of Play (for U9+)
- RFU FUNdamentals (for U7-U8)
- Keep Your Boots On (KYBO) methodology
